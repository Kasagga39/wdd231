M.J Electronics - Website Plan

Project Site Plan Document

Site Name

M.J Electronics

This name represents the business directly and clearly communicates the focus on electronics products and services. The initials "M.J" give the business a personal touch while "Electronics" clearly defines the industry.

Optional domain availability: mj-electronics.com

Site Purpose

The M.J Electronics website will serve as an informational hub for customers interested in consumer electronics, accessories, and repair services. The site will:

  • Showcase available electronics products and accessories
  • Provide detailed information about repair and troubleshooting services
  • Offer an overview of the store's history and mission
  • Enable customers to contact the business for inquiries or service requests

Scenarios

These scenarios represent questions from potential customers that the website should address:

Scenario 1: "I need to replace my phone screen. Does M.J Electronics offer screen repair services, and what is the typical turnaround time?"

Scenario 2: "I'm looking for charging cables and phone accessories. What brands and types of accessories does M.J Electronics carry, and are there any current promotions?"

Scenario 3: "My laptop is running slowly and I'm not sure what's wrong. Does M.J Electronics offer diagnostic services, and how much do they cost?"

Color Scheme

The selected color scheme for M.J Electronics website:

Primary #0056b3
Secondary #333333
Accent #ff9900
Background #f8f9fa
Light #ffffff

Color Applications:

  • Primary (#0056b3): Headings, navigation elements, primary buttons
  • Secondary (#333333): Body text, footer information
  • Accent (#ff9900): Call-to-action buttons, highlights, important notifications
  • Background (#f8f9fa): Main page background
  • Light (#ffffff): Content sections, cards, header background

Typography

The selected fonts for the M.J Electronics website:

Arial - For Headings

Arial is a clean, professional sans-serif font that works well for headings and navigation elements. It provides good readability and a modern appearance.

Segoe UI - For Body Text

Segoe UI is a humanist sans-serif typeface known for its excellent readability on screens. It will be used for all body text, paragraphs, and general content.

Font Applications:

  • Arial: All headings (h1-h6), navigation menu, buttons
  • Segoe UI: Body text, paragraphs, lists, form labels
