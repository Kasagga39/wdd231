About MJ Electronics & Energies

Empowering communities through sustainable technology since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015, MJ Electronics & Energies began as a small electronics repair shop in Masaka City. Recognizing the growing need for reliable power solutions in our community, we expanded our focus to include solar energy systems and sustainable power solutions.

Today, we serve residential, commercial, and institutional customers across Central Uganda, providing cutting-edge technology that reduces dependency on unstable grid power and minimizes environmental impact.
