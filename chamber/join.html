NP Membership Benefits

Our NP Membership is designed specifically for non-profit organizations with no membership fee.

  • Access to our online resource library
  • Listing in our non-profit directory
  • Quarterly newsletter with sector updates
  • Invitations to annual non-profit networking events
  • Access to grant opportunity database
  • Volunteer matching services

Cost: Free for qualifying non-profits

Bronze Membership Benefits

Our Bronze Membership offers essential benefits for growing organizations.

  • All NP Membership benefits
  • 10% discount on all training workshops
  • Access to member-only webinars
  • Business listing in our online directory
  • Monthly networking events
  • Access to industry reports
  • Member spotlight opportunity (quarterly drawing)

Silver Membership Benefits

Our Silver Membership provides enhanced benefits for established organizations.

  • Priority registration for events

  • Access to premium industry research
  • Member spotlight opportunity (biannual)
  • Invitation to exclusive roundtable discussions
