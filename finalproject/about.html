Our Story

Founded in 2015, MJ Electronics & Energies began as a small electronics repair shop in Masaka City. What started as a humble venture with a single workbench has grown into a leading provider of sustainable energy solutions in Central Uganda.

Our founder, Joseph Mayanja, recognized the critical need for reliable power in a region plagued by frequent electricity outages. This insight sparked our transformation from a repair shop to a comprehensive energy solutions provider.

2015
Founded as MJ Electronics repair shop in Masaka
2017
Expanded into solar energy systems installation
2020
Launched commercial solar solutions division
2023
Became certified installer for leading solar brands
"Our mission is not just to sell products, but to empower communities with sustainable energy solutions that transform lives and businesses."

— Joseph Mayanja, Founder & CEO
